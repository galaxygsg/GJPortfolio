Why Purdue?

There are a few reasons why I chose Purdue. First being, the have one of the best Video Game Development programs in the nation. Second, the networking the university has is unmatched among their competitors. Finally, they are a part of the Big Ten Conference, and compete in the most difficult league in the NCAA DI division.

What engines are you proficient in?

I am the most confident in the Unreal engine. The many things the engine allows you to do while being entirely free to use is incredible. I aim to further improve my knowledge in the engine in the future.

What kinds of games have you been a part of, Jason?

I have been on the art concept team of a mobile game called Fight Knights: Revival. Its similar to Game of War: Fire Age or Clash of Clans with its own twist. I was responsible for the characters in the art concepts. I also created a card memory game called Cards of Myth in Construct3, a HTML/JS game engine. I made all the art and scripting.

You said you have interest in sports, what kind of sprots are you in to Jason?

My big sports I like a lot are football and basketball. I am from Florida, so I have always rocked with the University of Florida Gators. Go Gators! Of course, now that I am a student at Purdue, I am forever a Boilermaker! Professionally, I love the Boston Celtics and the Jacksonville Jaguars, Yankees are great too.

Giancarlos you mentioned that you worked on The Mimic, how was that?

Well, in my specific role, I had a hand in creating the 3D models and animations while maintaining the atmosphere. It was a great foot in the door into the world of game development! My hopes are to land a career in this industry and this is definitely a foot in the door.

How is your time at the Purdue University, even as a Game Development Major?

My time has been unreal. I truly believe that Purdue University has one of the most unique cultures to date, and now I can experience that. The Boilermaker Special is a lifestyle no other school can match. From the cheers from Mackey and Ross-Ade to the untouchable education in all fields cannot be found elsewhere
